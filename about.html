Our Story

Born from a
love of light
and craft.

Lumina was founded in Singapore in 2018 by jeweller Isabelle Tan, driven by a belief that truly beautiful jewellery should be both exquisitely made and ethically responsible.

How it began

A goldsmith's
daughter, a
lifelong dream

Isabelle Tan grew up watching her father work in his Chinatown jewellery workshop. At age eight, she sat beside him polishing rings. At eighteen, she left Singapore to study goldsmithing at Antwerp's Royal Academy of Fine Arts. She returned fifteen years later with a vision: to create jewellery that honours both tradition and conscience.

Lumina launched with five pieces and a small studio. Today, we have a team of eight artisans, a boutique on Orchard Road, and customers who have trusted us with their most precious moments — engagements, anniversaries, and everything in between.

Every decision we make — from the source of our gold to our packaging — is guided by that same original intention: to make something truly worth keeping.

How we work

From first sketch
to finished heirloom

01

Design

Every piece begins as a hand-drawn sketch. Our designers work from concept to refined drawings before any metal is touched.

02

Sourcing

We select only conflict-free diamonds and certified coloured stones from suppliers who meet our strict ethical and environmental standards.

03

Crafting

Our Singapore artisans hand-fabricate each piece using recycled precious metals. Settings are measured to 0.1mm for perfect stone security.

04

Finishing

Final polish, quality inspection, and hallmarking. Each piece is presented in our signature sustainable packaging, ready to be gifted or cherished.

Our People

The hands
behind every piece

Our team of eight artisans brings together expertise from across Singapore, Malaysia, India, and Europe. Each person has dedicated years to mastering a specific craft — from stone setting to engraving to chain-making.

Isabelle Tan

Founder & Head Designer

Goldsmithing graduate of Antwerp Royal Academy. 20 years of experience designing fine jewellery across Singapore and Europe.

Rajan Pillai

What we stand for

Our values,
built into every piece

◆

Ethical Sourcing

We only work with suppliers who comply with the Kimberley Process for diamonds and adhere to fair-trade principles for coloured stones. No exceptions.

◇

Sustainable Metals

All our gold and silver is 100% recycled, certified by the Responsible Jewellery Council. We offset the remaining carbon footprint of every shipment.

✦

Genuine Craftsmanship

We never outsource to factories. Every piece is made in our Singapore studio by a named artisan who takes personal pride in what they create.
